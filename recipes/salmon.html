Honey Soy Glazed Salmon

Description

Salmon are flavorful, nutrient-rich fish known for their pink-orange flesh and delicate texture. Packed with omega-3 fatty acids, protein, and essential vitamins, salmon are both delicious and heart-healthy. Popular in cuisines worldwide, they can be enjoyed grilled, baked, smoked, or served raw as sushi.
  • 2 almon filets
  • 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh herbs
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. Prepare the salmon: Place fillets on a baking sheet lined with foil or parchment. Season: Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle salt and pepper, add minced garlic, and top with lemon slices and herbs.
  3. Bake: Cook for 12â€“15 minutes, or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  4. Serve: Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top and enjoy!
