Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a comforting, classic dish featuring tender elbow-shaped pasta enveloped in a rich, creamy cheese sauce. The sauce is typically made from a blend of melted cheeses—most often cheddar—mixed with butter, milk or cream, and a touch of seasoning, creating a smooth, velvety texture that clings perfectly to each noodle. The dish can be served soft and gooey straight from the stove or baked until the top forms a golden, slightly crispy crust. Its flavor is warm and indulgent, with a perfect balance of savory, cheesy goodness and subtle buttery richness, making it a beloved comfort food across generations.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of macaroni
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flower
  • 2 tbsp salt and pepper
  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions and drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter, stir in flour, and cook 1–2 minutes.
  3. Gradually add milk, stirring until thickened.
  4. Remove from heat, stir in cheese until melted.
  5. Mix cheese sauce with macaroni, season with salt and pepper, and serve.
